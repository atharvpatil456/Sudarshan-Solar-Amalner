Solar Grid Connected System

Product Information

Grid-connected solar systems allow you to generate your own electricity while remaining connected to the utility grid. These systems can feed excess power back to the grid, earning you credits through net metering.

Key Features:

  • High-efficiency solar panels
  • Grid-tie inverter
  • Net metering capability
  • Monitoring system
  • No battery storage required

Technical Specifications

  • System Size: 1kW to 100kW
  • Panel Type: Monocrystalline/Polycrystalline
  • Inverter: String/Central Inverter
  • Efficiency: 18-22%
  • Warranty: 25 Years on Panels
  • Payback Period: 4-6 Years
  • Lifespan: 25+ Years

Price Range:

₹40,000 - ₹60,000 per kW (installed)

Solar Off-Grid System

Product Information

Off-grid solar systems provide complete energy independence, perfect for remote locations or as backup power solutions. These systems include battery storage to ensure power availability 24/7.

Key Features:

  • Complete energy independence
  • Battery storage system
  • Charge controller
  • Pure sine wave inverter
  • Backup power capability

Technical Specifications

  • System Size: 500W to 10kW
  • Battery Type: Lithium-ion/Lead-acid
  • Backup Time: 8-24 hours
  • Inverter: Pure Sine Wave
  • Warranty: 5 Years System
  • Efficiency: 85-95%

Price Range:

₹80,000 - ₹1,50,000 per kW (with batteries)
